Key Findings

Student Level Analysis

  • Graduate students show highest satisfaction (4.2/5)
  • High school students have longest sessions (22.3 min)
  • Undergraduate students represent 65% of users

Discipline Performance

  • History and Engineering lead satisfaction
  • Computer Science has highest completion rates
  • Psychology shows moderate satisfaction

Task Type Effectiveness

  • Homework Help achieves highest satisfaction
  • Writing tasks are most common (35%)
  • Coding tasks show strong completion rates

AI Assistance Impact

  • Higher assistance levels (4-5) increase satisfaction
  • Optimal levels vary by task and student level
  • High satisfaction correlates with reuse
