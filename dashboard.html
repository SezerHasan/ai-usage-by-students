Key Findings

Student Level Analysis

  • All student levels show similar satisfaction (3.41-3.43/5)
  • High school students have longest sessions (20.0 min)
  • Undergraduate students represent 59.8% of users

Discipline Performance

  • Biology leads satisfaction (3.45/5)
  • History shows strong performance (3.44/5)
  • Psychology has moderate satisfaction (3.43/5)

Task Type Effectiveness

  • Coding achieves highest satisfaction (3.46/5)
  • Homework Help shows strong performance (3.43/5)
  • Writing tasks are most common (31%)

AI Assistance Impact

  • Higher assistance levels (4-5) increase satisfaction
  • Strong correlation (r = 0.776) with satisfaction
  • Level 5 achieves 4.67/5 satisfaction
